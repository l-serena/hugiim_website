April 4, 2026 · Harvard Science Center

Women+ in Math and Statistics (WiMS)

Harvard Undergraduate Gender Inclusivity in Math is excited to announce our annual Women+ in Math and Statistics (WiMS) conference! This year’s conference will be held on April 4th, 2026 in the Harvard Science Center. WiMS aims to spotlight the experiences and accomplishments of gender minorities in mathematics and statistics. We welcome all undergraduate and graduate students interested in math and related fields to attend!

WiMS presents a unique opportunity to hear from accomplished speakers and panelists in academia and in industry. The conference features Prof. Judith Lok and Prof. Isabel Vogt as keynote speakers, and includes panels on experiences in academia and industry.
