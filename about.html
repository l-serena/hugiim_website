Our Story

Who We Are

Harvard Undergraduate Gender Inclusivity in Mathematics (HUGIIM) is a Harvard undergraduate organization dedicated to creating a more equitable and welcoming environment in the math community.

We host talks, workshops, social events, and mentorship programs that connect students across all levels — from first-years exploring calculus to senior thesis writers — united by a shared love of mathematics.

HUGIIM works closely with the Harvard Department of Mathematics.
